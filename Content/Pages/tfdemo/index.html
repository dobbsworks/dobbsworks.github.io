Welcome to the team!

TeamFantastik is a team filled with creative makers that love making fun levels with a mixture of shell tricks and fun platforming in a "RomHack" style. Our team welcomes anyone who loves to build or play levels that are well-made, intuitive, and fun to play.

Get Registered

Anyone can play and enjoy these Fantastik levels, but if you want to start tracking your clears and climbing the leaderboard, you'll need to get registered on the Discord. Head on over to the #registration channel and use the command !register MAK-ERC-ODE.

Find levels

In the #bot-spam channel, type !random 0.5 to get a hand-picked warm-up level. Once you've cleared it, use !clear LEV-ELC-ODE to let us know you've cleared it. Add like to the command to let us know it's a great course. That's it!

Submit levels

When you feel like you have a good feel for what makes a level Fantastik, you can submit your own! Make sure to read through #level-rules and #banned-tech before submitting, then head over to #submit-levels. To get your level submitted for review, use !add STYLE LEV-ELC-ODE My level name (where STYLE is SMB, SMB3, etc.).
