GAMES DONE BADLY 2022

What is GDB?

Games Done Badly is a yearly marathon* of video game streams. Unlike other gaming marathons that focus on things like extraordinary skill and precise execution, GDB stands out by having no theme at all. Wow. It's more of an excuse to hang out and play video games for a couple of days.

There is the suggestion of a central theme in that games are played badly, which encompasses being bad at games (offense taken), playing games with some sort of handicap (challenge runs), or playing games that are considered bad (it was good back then, trust me).

When is GDB?

GDB 2022 will be held October 8th and 9th this year!
